Summary: Latitude vs Cloudiness

This graph is pretty even throughout when it comes to percentage of cloudiness based on latitude. We can see that the higher the latitude is though, the less sunlight is received and the cloudier it is.

Cloudiness percentages seem to be closer to either 0% or 100% as the latitude increases, and percentages between those 2 extemes do not have many cities within.
