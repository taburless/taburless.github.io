Summary: Latitude vs Wind Speed

As expected, wind speed has was not affected much by an increase or decrease in latitude. For the most part, wind speed stays between 0 and 7 mph. There are some outliers with higher wind speeds and both high and low latitudes.

There could potentially be more visible variations if we decreased the scale of the Y axis, and wind speeds on average are less than 8 mph and that is where many wind speeds fall normally.
