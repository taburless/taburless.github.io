Summary: Latitude vs Temperature

As expected, the temperature is highest at the equator because at 0 degrees latitude is where the sun shines the hardest. We can see that illustrated on the graph, as the peak of the curve in temperatures is shown between 0 and 25 degrees of latitude.

Also we can see as we head north, with latitude increasing, temperature is consistenly dropping.
